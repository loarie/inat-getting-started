What is iNaturalist?

iNaturalist helps you record what you see in nature and share with a community of naturalists. Along the way we hope you’ll learn more about what you saw and create research quality data for science. Here’s how it works...

Observe

Cullen sees an interesting snail on a hike in California. He records the encounter as an observation.

Identify

Susan is a zoologist in New York. She identifies Cullen’s observation as a Small Pointed Snail. This kind of snail is native to Europe, but is poorly known in California.

Learn

Michelle works at the Santa Clara Division of Agriculture. She uses Cullen’s observation in her research to understand and manage this new biological invasion.
